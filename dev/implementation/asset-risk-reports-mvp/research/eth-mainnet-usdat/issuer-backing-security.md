## Agent-context summary

USDat is Saturn's 6-decimal, permissioned stablecoin on Ethereum. Official docs say it is designed for 1:1 USD liquidity/settlement and is backed at launch by M0's `$M` tokenized U.S. Treasuries product. The onchain/admin artifact shows USDat is not an immutable, freely transferable ERC-20: it is a TransparentUpgradeableProxy with whitelist enabled, immediate EOA-controlled compliance roles for pause/freeze/forced-transfer/whitelist, and an immediate EOA ProxyAdmin owner path. Saturn's transparency page states USDat capital is held directly in the USDat smart contract and verifiable onchain, but this recovery pass did not independently verify the `$M` reserve composition, custody/legal terms, or audit PDF scope against the current deployed implementation. Treat issuer/backing as documented enough for dossier synthesis but `review_required` before clean collateral acceptance or automated execution.

## 2. Issuer / protocol and business model

### Mechanism

Saturn docs describe USDat as Saturn's fully collateralized stablecoin for liquidity and settlement, maintaining a 1:1 peg to the U.S. dollar and mintable/redeemable with USDC through the Saturn interface. They state that at launch reserves are 100% `$M`, M0's tokenized U.S. Treasuries product. Sources: S2 medium.

The exact-token onchain/admin artifact shows the live USDat contract is a permissioned and upgradeable token with whitelist, freeze, forced-transfer, pause, asset-cap, and AccessControl-style role surfaces. Sources: S1/S4 high.

### Business and yield model

- USDat does not directly accrue yield to holders according to Saturn's USDat overview. Yield generated by the underlying `$M` reserves flows to Saturn's protocol revenue vault; users seeking yield exposure are directed to sUSDat. Source: S2 medium.
- The token is intended as a settlement/liquidity stablecoin rather than a yield-bearing share token. Source: S2 medium.
- Business model dependencies include Saturn onboarding/compliance, `$M` availability/liquidity, protocol revenue capture from underlying reserves, and Saturn-controlled mint/redeem infrastructure. Source: S2/S3 medium, S1 high for onchain controls.

### Control and redemption access summary

- Access/compliance: Saturn docs say USDat is permissioned and only addresses that completed onboarding can mint, redeem, or hold USDat. Source: S2 medium.
- Onchain controls: whitelist is currently enabled; compliance EOA can pause, freeze, forced-transfer frozen accounts, and manage whitelist. Source: S1 high.
- Direct redemption: docs say redemptions return USDC to the user's wallet through the Saturn interface. This recovery pass did not test the frontend, legal eligibility, or a live redeem quote. Source: S2 medium; `missing_behavior: review_required` for user-specific eligibility.

## 3. Backing, NAV, and exposure map

`nav_model: 1:1 reserve / tokenized-treasury-backed issuer stablecoin / permissioned wrapper`

### Backing model

- Official docs: USDat is backed by `$M`, M0's tokenized U.S. Treasuries product, with launch reserves described as 100% `$M`. Source: S2 medium.
- Official transparency page: all USDat capital is held directly in the USDat smart contract and verifiable onchain at any time. Source: S3 medium.
- Onchain source: USDat implementation is an M-extension/JMI-style token with whitelist wrapping/unwrapping hooks. This supports the framing that USDat is tied to an underlying wrapped asset path rather than an ordinary issuer-free ERC-20. Source: S1/S4 high.

### NAV / reserve caveats

- This pass did not independently verify M0 `$M` reserve reports, custody, redemption terms, or M0-specific smart-contract controls. Because `$M` is the backing asset named by Saturn, those details remain material for production collateral-quality analysis. `missing_behavior: review_required`.

- NAV can diverge from practical exit value if Saturn onboarding, whitelist status, USDat pause/freeze, `$M` liquidity, or USDC redemption capacity is impaired. `missing_behavior: review_required` for clean ranking; `block_automation` for real exits without live Preview/eligibility checks.
